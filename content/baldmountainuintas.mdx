# Bald Mountain Uintas Climbing Guide

Bald Mountain in the Uintas offers solid granite climbing with a mix of sport and trad routes across multiple crags. The area features quality rock and varied terrain, making it a worthy destination for climbers looking to explore beyond the Wasatch. Access involves a decent approach, so come prepared for a full day out.
