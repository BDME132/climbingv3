# Black Rocks St. George Climbing Guide

Black Rocks delivers quality basalt sport climbing and bouldering just minutes from St. George. The dark volcanic rock offers excellent friction and interesting movement, with routes spanning from mellow warm-ups to serious projects. Access is straightforward, but respect private property boundaries and pack out all trash to keep this area open.
