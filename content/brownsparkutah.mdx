# Browns Park Utah Climbing Guide

Browns Park delivers quality sandstone bouldering in a remote corner of northeastern Utah. The area features scattered boulder fields with everything from mellow warm-ups to world-class test pieces, making it worth the drive for boulderers looking to explore beyond the usual suspects. Access is generally straightforward, but come prepared for variable conditions and limited amenities.
