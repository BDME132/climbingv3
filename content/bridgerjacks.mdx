# Bridger Jacks Climbing Guide

Bridger Jacks delivers some of Utah's most striking desert tower climbing, with clean Wingate sandstone cracks and stunning exposure. The area attracts climbers looking for classic desert adventures and technical crack climbing in a more remote setting than the busy Moab walls. Access requires a decent drive on dirt roads, so plan accordingly and check conditions.
