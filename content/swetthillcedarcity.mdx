# Swett Hill Cedar City Climbing Guide

Swett Hill offers a solid mix of sport climbing and bouldering on quality sandstone just outside Cedar City. The area sees consistent traffic from locals and visitors looking for moderate routes and fun boulder problems in a scenic desert setting. Access is straightforward, but respect private property boundaries and pack out all trash.
