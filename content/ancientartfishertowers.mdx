# Ancient Art Fisher Towers Climbing Guide

Fisher Towers delivers some of Utah's most iconic desert tower climbing, with Ancient Art being the crown jewel that draws climbers from around the world. The area offers everything from mellow multi-pitch adventures to serious aid routes on questionable rock. Access is straightforward via the Fisher Towers Road, but expect crowds on weekends and during peak season.
