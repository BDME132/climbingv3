# Pfeifferhorn Climbing Guide

Pfeifferhorn offers a solid mix of alpine routes and sport climbing across multiple walls in the Wasatch. The area is known for quality traditional climbing on the peak itself, plus developed sport routes on walls like Purple Wall and JHCOB Wall. Access can be seasonal depending on snow conditions, so check current trail conditions before heading up.
