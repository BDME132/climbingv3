# Tusher Canyon Climbing Guide

Tusher Canyon delivers classic desert tower climbing with a mix of splitter cracks and technical face routes on solid Wingate sandstone. The area offers everything from mellow 5.8 trad lines to burly 5.12 sport routes, plus some quality bouldering around the iconic formations. Access is straightforward via the Tusher Canyon road, making it a solid choice for multi-day desert missions.
