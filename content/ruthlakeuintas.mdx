# Ruth Lake Uintas Climbing Guide

Ruth Lake offers solid sport and trad climbing on quality quartzite in a beautiful alpine setting. The area features three main walls with routes spanning from beginner-friendly 5.6s to challenging 5.12s, making it a great destination for mixed-ability groups. Access requires a moderate hike, so plan accordingly and check seasonal conditions.
