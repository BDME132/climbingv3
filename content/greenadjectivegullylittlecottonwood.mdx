# Green Adjective Gully Little Cottonwood Climbing Guide

Green Adjective Gully offers some of Little Cottonwood's most varied climbing, from mellow trad lines to burly boulder problems scattered throughout the drainage. The area sees steady traffic for good reason—quality rock, diverse grades, and enough routes to keep you busy for multiple sessions. Check current access conditions before heading up, as seasonal restrictions can affect parking and approach options.
