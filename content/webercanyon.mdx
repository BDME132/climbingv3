# Weber Canyon Climbing Guide

Weber Canyon offers solid traditional and sport climbing on quality rock, with a strong concentration of beginner-friendly routes that make it perfect for building skills. The Train Hop Area dominates the scene here, serving up everything from mellow 5.4 trad leads to classic 5.7 adventures. Access is straightforward, making this a go-to spot when you want good climbing without the approach drama.
