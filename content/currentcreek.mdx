# Current Creek Climbing Guide

Current Creek delivers classic Indian Creek-style crack climbing with that perfect desert sandstone everyone comes to Utah for. The routes here lean technical and sustained, making it a solid spot for intermediate and advanced trad climbers looking to test their jamming skills. Access is straightforward, but come prepared with a full rack of cams.
