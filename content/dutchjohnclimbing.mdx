# Dutch John Climbing Guide

Dutch John offers solid sandstone climbing in northeast Utah, with everything from mellow trad lines to technical sport routes scattered across multiple formations. The area sees fewer crowds than Moab but delivers quality stone and varied terrain. Most crags require short approaches, making it perfect for efficient sending sessions.
