# The Dihedrals Little Cottonwood Climbing Guide

The Dihedrals offers some of the most varied climbing in Little Cottonwood Canyon, with everything from mellow trad lines to burly sport routes and world-class bouldering. The granite here is solid, the approaches are reasonable, and you'll find climbs for every skill level scattered across multiple distinct areas. Just remember that Little Cottonwood has seasonal closures for wildlife, so check current access before heading up.
