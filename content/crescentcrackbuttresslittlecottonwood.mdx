# Crescent Crack Buttress Little Cottonwood Climbing Guide

Crescent Crack Buttress offers some of Little Cottonwood's most varied climbing, from mellow trad lines to burly sport routes and quality bouldering. The crag sits conveniently close to the road with good access, making it a go-to spot for everything from warm-ups to serious projects. You'll find classic hand cracks, technical face climbing, and enough variety to keep any session interesting.
