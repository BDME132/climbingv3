# Nine Mile Canyon Climbing Guide

Nine Mile Canyon offers solid sandstone climbing with a mix of traditional and sport routes spread across several crags. The area is known for quality beginner and intermediate climbing on featured sandstone walls. Access is straightforward, but respect private property and petroglyphs in the area.
