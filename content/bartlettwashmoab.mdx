# Bartlett Wash Moab Climbing Guide

Bartlett Wash serves up some of the most demanding traditional climbing in the Moab area, with routes that push the limits of what's possible on desert sandstone. This is where you come to test yourself against world-class hard trad lines that have put Moab on the map for serious traditional climbers. Access is straightforward, but bring your A-game and a full rack.
