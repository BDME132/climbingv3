# Factory Butte Area Climbing Guide

Factory Butte offers some of Utah's most adventurous desert tower climbing, with long multi-pitch routes on striking sandstone formations. This is serious desert climbing territory where self-sufficiency and solid trad skills are essential. Access can be tricky, so check current conditions and regulations before heading out.
