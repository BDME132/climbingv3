# Cedar Breaks Climbing Guide

Cedar Breaks offers solid limestone sport and trad climbing in a stunning high-elevation setting. The area features everything from mellow beginner routes to world-class hard sport climbs, with quality rock and well-developed walls. Check current access conditions before heading up, as seasonal closures can affect some areas.
