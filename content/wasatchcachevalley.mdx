# Wasatch | Cache Valley Climbing Guide

Cache Valley offers solid limestone and quartzite climbing with routes spanning the grade spectrum, from confidence-building sport climbs to burly test pieces. The area sees less traffic than the Wasatch Front, making it a good option when you want quality stone without the crowds. Access is generally straightforward, but check current conditions as some areas may have seasonal restrictions.
