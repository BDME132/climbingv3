# South Six Shooter Climbing Guide

South Six Shooter offers classic desert tower climbing with splitter cracks and stellar exposure in the heart of Indian Creek country. The routes here are pure trad affairs that'll test your crack skills and gear placement on quality Wingate sandstone. Access is straightforward from the main Indian Creek corridor.
