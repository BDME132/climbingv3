# Antelope Island Climbing

Antelope Island offers solid bouldering and some trad routes with stunning views over the Great Salt Lake. Most of the action happens at Buffalo Point, where you'll find everything from beginner-friendly problems to world-class test pieces. Access is straightforward through the state park, though you'll need to pay the entrance fee.

---
