# Price Canyon Climbing Guide

Price Canyon delivers quality sandstone bouldering in a scenic desert setting just outside Helper, Utah. The area features everything from mellow warm-ups to burly projects, with most problems offering excellent movement on solid rock. Access is straightforward, but respect private property and pack out your trash.
