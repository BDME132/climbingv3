# Maple Grove Climbing Utah Climbing Guide

Maple Grove delivers solid sport climbing on quality rock with routes that'll test your technical skills. The crag offers a good mix of intermediate and harder lines, making it a solid choice when you want to push your grade or dial in movement on steeper terrain. Access is straightforward, but respect private property boundaries and pack out your trash.
