# Slate Canyon Climbing Guide

Slate Canyon offers solid bouldering on quality rock, with the Funeral Boulder area serving as the main attraction. Most climbers come here for the varied boulder problems ranging from beginner-friendly V1s to world-class V15 testpieces. Access is straightforward, making it a reliable option when you want to get on rock without the approach drama.
