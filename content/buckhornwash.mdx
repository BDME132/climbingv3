# Buckhorn Wash Climbing Guide

Buckhorn Wash offers solid desert tower climbing and some quality sport routes scattered across multiple walls. It's a good spot when you want something different from the usual Utah suspects, with decent rock quality and a mix of styles to keep things interesting. Access is straightforward but respect private property boundaries in the area.
