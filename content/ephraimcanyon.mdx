# Ephraim Canyon Climbing Guide

Ephraim Canyon offers solid bouldering on quality sandstone with a good mix of problems for different skill levels. The area features two main zones - Dirt Bag Boulders and The Corral - both delivering classic Utah stone with varied movement styles. It's a great spot for working projects or getting in a solid session without the crowds of more popular areas.
