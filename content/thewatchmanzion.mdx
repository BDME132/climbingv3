# The Watchman Zion Climbing Guide

The Watchman offers some of Zion's most accessible yet challenging climbing, with everything from mellow scrambles to world-class boulder problems. The area is known for its solid Navajo sandstone and relatively short approaches, making it perfect for day sessions. Note that climbing regulations in Zion can be strict, so check current permit requirements before heading out.
