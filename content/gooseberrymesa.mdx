# Gooseberry Mesa Climbing Guide

Gooseberry Mesa offers solid sandstone climbing with stunning red rock formations and desert views. It's a great spot for traditional climbing on quality rock, though route selection can be limited. Access is straightforward, making it a solid choice for a desert climbing day trip.
