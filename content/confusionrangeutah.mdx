# Confusion Range Utah Climbing Guide

The Confusion Range offers some of Utah's most diverse climbing, from beginner-friendly trad lines to challenging sport routes across multiple distinct walls. The area draws climbers for its quality limestone and quartzite, varied terrain, and relatively uncrowded feel compared to other Utah destinations.
