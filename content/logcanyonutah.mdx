# Log Canyon Utah Climbing Guide

Log Canyon offers solid sport climbing on quality sandstone with routes that won't punish you for being human. It's a great spot for working technique on moderate grades, and the approach is mercifully short. Access is straightforward, but respect private property boundaries and pack out your trash.
