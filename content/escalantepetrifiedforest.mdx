# Escalante Petrified Forest Climbing Guide

The Escalante Petrified Forest area offers diverse climbing across multiple walls and formations, from mellow trad lines to steep sport routes. The area features quality Navajo sandstone with everything from beginner-friendly cracks to challenging overhangs, plus solid bouldering opportunities. Access varies by area, so check current conditions and regulations before heading out.
