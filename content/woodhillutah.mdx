# Wood Hill Utah Climbing Guide

Wood Hill offers solid bouldering and some quality trad lines in a relatively accessible setting. The area sees steady traffic from Salt Lake climbers looking for varied problems and classic crack climbing. Access is straightforward, making it a reliable option for weekend sessions.
