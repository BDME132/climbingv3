# Coffin Buttress Little Cottonwood Climbing Guide

Coffin Buttress offers a solid mix of trad and sport climbing on quality quartzite, with routes spanning from mellow 5.8 to burly 5.12+. The crag sits in Little Cottonwood Canyon and delivers the classic LCC experience—bomber rock, alpine starts, and routes that'll test your gear game. Access is straightforward, but check current canyon restrictions before heading up.
