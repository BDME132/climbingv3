# Lisa Falls Climbing Guide

Lisa Falls offers solid limestone sport and trad climbing in a scenic canyon setting. The area features multiple walls with routes spanning from beginner-friendly 5.4s to challenging 5.12 projects, making it a go-to spot for mixed-ability groups and anyone looking to dial in technique on quality stone.
