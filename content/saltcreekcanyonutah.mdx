# Salt Creek Canyon Utah Climbing Guide

Salt Creek Canyon offers solid sport climbing on quality sandstone in a scenic desert setting. The area is known for its accessible routes on the Chokecherry Dike, making it a great spot for working through the grades from 5.5 to 5.9. Access is straightforward, but check current regulations as this area can have seasonal restrictions.
