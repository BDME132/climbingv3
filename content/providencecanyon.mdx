# Providence Canyon Climbing Guide

Providence Canyon offers a solid mix of sport and trad routes on quality rock, plus some fun bouldering for when you want to stay close to the ground. It's a great spot for working through grades or just getting some movement in without the crowds. The "Close to Home" wall lives up to its name with easily accessible routes right off the approach.

---
