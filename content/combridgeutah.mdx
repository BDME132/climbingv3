# Comb Ridge Utah Climbing Guide

Comb Ridge offers classic desert tower climbing and crack systems stretching along a dramatic sandstone ridge in southeastern Utah. The area delivers quality traditional routes on solid rock with that quintessential Utah desert experience. Access can involve significant approaches, so plan accordingly and respect the sensitive desert environment.
