# Wasatch | Ogden Valley Climbing Guide

The Wasatch around Ogden Valley serves up everything from mellow trad lines to world-class sport climbing, with crags scattered from Ogden Canyon to the high alpine. You'll find solid beginner terrain at 9th Street and Hole in the Rock, plus some of Utah's hardest sends at Hardware Wall. Access is generally straightforward, but check current conditions for the higher elevation areas.
