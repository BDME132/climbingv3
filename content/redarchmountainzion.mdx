# Red Arch Mountain Zion Climbing Guide

Red Arch Mountain offers some of Zion's most rewarding traditional climbing on solid Navajo sandstone. The routes here demand solid trad skills and route-finding abilities, but reward you with incredible exposure and classic desert tower climbing. Access requires a permit and approach through the main canyon.
