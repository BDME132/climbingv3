# Zeus Tower Canyonlands Climbing Guide

Zeus Tower and the surrounding Taylor Canyon area offer some of Utah's most striking desert tower climbing, with everything from accessible multi-pitch trad routes to burly single-pitch challenges. The area is known for quality Wingate and Navajo sandstone formations that provide excellent friction and varied crack systems. Access is straightforward via Taylor Canyon Road, making it a solid choice for both day trips and longer desert climbing objectives.
