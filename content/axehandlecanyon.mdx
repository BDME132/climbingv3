# Axe Handle Canyon Climbing Guide

Axe Handle Canyon is a solid bouldering destination that delivers quality problems on good rock. Most of the action centers around The Saloon, which offers everything from mellow warm-ups to burly projects. Access is straightforward, making it a go-to spot when you want to get on rock without the approach drama.
