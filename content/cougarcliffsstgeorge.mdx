# Cougar Cliffs St. George Climbing Guide

Cougar Cliffs delivers solid sport climbing on quality sandstone with routes spanning 5.7 to 5.11+, plus some decent bouldering. The area sees consistent traffic thanks to its accessible approach and variety of grades that work well for groups with mixed abilities. Access is straightforward, but respect private property boundaries and pack out your trash.
