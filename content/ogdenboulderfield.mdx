# Ogden Boulder Field Climbing Guide

Ogden Boulder Field is a sprawling collection of quality quartzite boulders offering everything from mellow warm-ups to world-class test pieces. The area draws climbers for its diverse problems, solid rock, and relatively quick access from the Wasatch Front. Note that this is a popular area that can get crowded on weekends, so consider hitting it during weekdays or off-peak hours.
