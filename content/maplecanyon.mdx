# Maple Canyon Climbing Guide

Maple Canyon serves up some of Utah's most unique sport climbing on distinctive cobble conglomerate rock. The textured holds and varied angles make for engaging movement, while the canyon's collection of walls offers everything from mellow warm-ups to serious projects. Access is straightforward, but respect private property and seasonal closures.
