# Maple Canyon Climbing Guide

Maple Canyon is Utah's conglomerate climbing paradise, offering everything from beginner-friendly sport routes to world-class hard sends on unique cobblestone rock. The canyon's diverse crags provide excellent year-round climbing, with shaded walls for summer sessions and sunny exposure for winter days. Access is straightforward, but respect private property and follow Leave No Trace principles to keep this gem open.
