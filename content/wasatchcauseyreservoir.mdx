# Wasatch | Causey Reservoir Climbing Guide

Causey Reservoir offers solid limestone sport climbing with a handful of quality walls scattered around the area. The climbing here tends toward the technical and steep, with Serendipity Wall serving up some of the area's hardest sends. Access is straightforward, but respect private property and pack out your trash.
