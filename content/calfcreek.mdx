# Calf Creek Climbing Guide

Calf Creek offers quality sport climbing on Navajo sandstone in a stunning desert setting near Escalante. The area is known for its scenic approach and solid rock, making it a worthy stop for climbers exploring southern Utah. Access is straightforward via the popular hiking trail to Lower Calf Creek Falls.
