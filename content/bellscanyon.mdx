# Bells Canyon Climbing Guide

Bells Canyon offers some of Utah's most classic traditional climbing on solid quartzite towers and domes. The area is known for its multi-pitch adventures and technical face climbing, with routes ranging from mellow beginner lines to serious test pieces. Access requires a moderate hike, so come prepared for a full day in the mountains.
