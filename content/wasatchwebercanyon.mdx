# Wasatch | Weber Canyon Climbing Guide

Weber Canyon offers a solid mix of traditional climbing and bouldering just off I-84, making it a convenient option for Salt Lake climbers looking to get on rock without the drive to the Cottonwoods. The area is known for its train-themed route names and varied rock quality, with everything from mellow trad lines to challenging sport routes. Access is straightforward, but be mindful of private property and seasonal closures.
