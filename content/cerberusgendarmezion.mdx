# Cerberus Gendarme Zion Climbing Guide

Cerberus Gendarme offers some of Zion's most varied traditional climbing, from mellow crack systems to burly overhangs that'll test your limits. The sandstone here rewards solid technique and a full rack, with routes that range from perfect beginner outings to world-class test pieces. Access is straightforward from the main canyon, making it a go-to spot when you want quality trad without the epic approach.
