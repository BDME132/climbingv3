# Peruvian Buttress Little Cottonwood Climbing Guide

Peruvian Buttress offers solid granite sport and trad climbing in Little Cottonwood Canyon's classic alpine setting. The area sees less traffic than some of the canyon's more famous walls, making it a good option when you want quality routes without the crowds. Access follows standard Little Cottonwood protocols—respect private property and seasonal wildlife closures.
