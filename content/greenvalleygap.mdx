# Green Valley Gap Climbing Guide

Green Valley Gap offers a solid mix of beginner-friendly trad lines and sport routes that progress nicely into intermediate territory. It's a great spot for working your way up the grades or introducing newer climbers to outdoor rock, with generally good rock quality and straightforward approaches.
