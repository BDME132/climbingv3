# Echo Tower Fisher Towers Climbing Guide

Fisher Towers delivers some of Utah's most iconic desert tower climbing, with towering sandstone spires that demand solid trad skills and a head for exposure. The area spans multiple formations from beginner-friendly multi-pitch routes to world-class aid lines that'll test your commitment. Access is straightforward via the Fisher Towers Road, but come prepared for approach hikes and classic desert conditions.
