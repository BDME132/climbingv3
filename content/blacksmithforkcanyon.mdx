# Blacksmith Fork Canyon Climbing Guide

Blacksmith Fork Canyon delivers solid limestone sport climbing with a handful of trad options, tucked into the mountains northeast of Logan. The rock quality is generally good and the route variety keeps things interesting, from mellow slabs to steep technical walls. Access is straightforward, but respect private property and seasonal wildlife closures.
