# Hayden Peak Uintas Climbing Guide

Hayden Peak in the Uintas offers a solid mix of traditional climbing and bouldering in a high-alpine setting that feels worlds away from the desert crags. The area is known for quality granite routes with good protection and some challenging boulder problems that'll test your skills. Access requires a decent hike, so come prepared for a full day adventure.
